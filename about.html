Our Story

My Amazspace was born out of frustration with misleading product reviews and sponsored content that prioritizes profits over people. As consumers ourselves, we know how overwhelming it can be to navigate endless product options and contradictory reviews online.

Founded in 2025 by a team of tech enthusiasts and product testing experts, we set out to create a different kind of review site. One where transparency isn't just a buzzword, but the foundation of everything we do.
