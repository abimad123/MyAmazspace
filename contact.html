Frequently Asked Questions

How do you choose which products to review?

We select products based on reader requests, market trends, and our own research into what's popular or innovative. We prioritize products that our audience is actually interested in buying.

Do you accept products from manufacturers?

No, we purchase all products with our own money at full retail price. This ensures our reviews remain completely unbiased and honest.

How long do you test products?

Testing periods vary by product type, but we typically use products for several weeks to months before writing our reviews. We want to understand long-term performance, not just first impressions.

Can you review a specific product I suggest?

Absolutely! We love reader suggestions. While we can't guarantee we'll review every suggested product, we consider all suggestions and prioritize those that align with our audience's interests.
